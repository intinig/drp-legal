Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-06-17

Short version: the Bot has no database and stores no personal data. It reads the command you send, replies, and forgets it.

1. What the Bot processes

When you use a slash command, Discord sends the Bot the data needed to handle it: your Discord user ID, the command and any arguments you provide (for example an item name, or the item text you paste into /paste), and the server/channel where you invoked it. This happens only when you actively use a command.

2. How it is used

That input is used solely to generate an immediate reply. It is processed in memory and discarded once the response is sent. It is never written to a database, because there isn't one.

3. What the Bot does not do

  • No persistent storage of your messages, inputs, or IDs.
  • No profiling, advertising, analytics, or behavioural tracking.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of your data with anyone.

4. Logging

The server keeps minimal, short-lived operational logs (errors and aggregate counts) to keep the Bot running. These are not used to identify or profile individuals and are not shared.

5. Third parties

You interact with the Bot through Discord, whose handling of your data is governed by Discord's Privacy Policy. The Bot fetches public market data from poe.ninja; it does not send any of your information to poe.ninja.

6. Hosting & location

The Bot runs on a private server hosted in the European Union (Germany).

7. Data retention & your rights

Because nothing is stored, there is no personal data to retain, export, or delete. If you have questions or concerns, contact us (below) and we will help.

8. Children

The Bot is not directed at anyone below Discord's minimum age requirement for their country.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated; the "Effective" date above reflects the latest version.
